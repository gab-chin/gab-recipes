Rice-cooker Hainanese Chicken Rice

From Douglas Chau

Here is the simplest Hainanese chicken and rice recipe you can make all in your rice cooker that delivers most of the results without needing to boil and poach a whole chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
    • 450g chicken thighs bone-in
    • 2 tsp sesame oil
    • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Rice
    • 2 cups jasmine rice
    • 6 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1/2 inch ginger, minced
    • 2 scallions, chopped
    • 2 cups chicken broth, slightly less than normal
    • 1 tsp chicken bouillon
    • optional, 1/2 tsp tumeric powder
  • Ginger Scallion Sauce
    • 1/2 inch ginger, minced
    • 1 green onion, minced
    • 1 tsp sesame oil
    • salt to taste
    • 3 tbsp neutral oil
  • Chili Sauce
    • 6 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1/2 inch ginger, minced
    • 2 tbsp sambal
    • 2 tbsp siracha
    • 2 tbsp chicken stock
    • 1 tbsp lime juice
    • 2 tsp sugar
  • optional, sliced cucumbers for freshness
